Description of Service

MenuPics is an AI-powered menu scanning app that extracts dish information from photos of restaurant menus and matches each dish to real food photographs from third-party image search services. The app is intended as a visual aid for ordering and is not a substitute for ingredient labels, allergen information, or restaurant guidance.

Accuracy Disclaimer

Dish photos shown in the app are sourced from third-party image search providers, including Bing Image Search, Pexels, and Wikimedia Commons, and may not exactly represent the dish as prepared by the specific restaurant you are visiting. AI-extracted dish names, descriptions, prices, and categories are best-effort and may contain errors. Always confirm details with the restaurant before ordering.
