Accuracy Disclaimer

Fiber values come from public nutrition databases (including USDA FoodData Central and Open Food Facts) and, for AI meal scanning, from automated food identification and portion estimation. AI-estimated foods, portions, and fiber amounts are best-effort and may contain errors. Photographs cannot reliably determine hidden ingredients, preparation methods, or exact portion sizes. Always review and adjust scanned results, and do not rely on the app for precise nutritional intake.
