Questions before you sign up.

What is CloserLab?
An iPhone app that lets salespeople practice handling objections against realistic AI buyers. Describe what you sell, and the app generates buyers who push back like real prospects. You roleplay the call in voice or text and get a coaching score afterward.
Why not just practice with a coworker?
A coworker is busy, goes easy on you, and can't roleplay 40 buyer types on demand at 11pm. CloserLab gives you an endless bench of buyers who stay in character, throw the objections you're weakest on, and score you honestly every time.
Do I need an account?
No. CloserLab needs no account, sign-in, name, email, or phone number to use. Your offer, buyer roster, and replay history live locally on your device.
Is there a voice mode?
Yes. You can talk through the call out loud and the AI buyer talks back, so you rehearse tone and delivery, not just the words. A text-only mode is there when you can't speak up.
When does it launch?
CloserLab is in development for iPhone. Join the waitlist and you'll get one email the day it goes live on the App Store.
