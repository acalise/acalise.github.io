The Approach

Six categories. One quiet challenge a day.

Most "confidence" apps are repackaged journals or motivational quote machines. Introvert focuses on doing — small, specific, doable acts of courage that compound. Pick a category or let the app pick. Either way, you get one challenge with a clear ask and a one-line tip so you always know what "doing it" looks like.

💬

Social Skills

Tiny conversational reps — say good morning to your barista, ask a coworker about their weekend, hold eye contact two seconds longer.

💼

Career Confidence

Small career moves — speak up in one meeting, send the cold email, ask for a 1:1 with your manager about growth.

🎤

Public Speaking

Build the muscle privately first — record yourself, present in a low-stakes meeting, take the floor for 60 seconds at standup.

🤝

Networking

The kind of networking that actually works for introverts — one DM, one warm intro request, one comment on someone's post.

✨

Self-Expression

Say the thing — share an opinion, post the thought, wear the outfit, send the message you've been drafting for three days.

🚪

Comfort Zone

Eat at a restaurant alone. Take the long walk. Try the class. Tiny pushes against the wall of "I don't usually do that."

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before downloading.

What is Introvert: Daily Courage?
Introvert is a daily courage app for quiet people. It delivers one small, specific challenge a day across six categories — social skills, career, public speaking, networking, self-expression, and comfort-zone pushes. Forgiving streaks, a home screen widget, 580+ hand-written challenges. iPhone only.
Is this a social anxiety app?
No. Social anxiety apps focus on CBT exercises, exposure hierarchies, and clinical assessments. Introvert is for personal growth — gentle, doable courage challenges that compound. It's not therapy. If you have clinical social anxiety, work with a therapist; many users use Introvert alongside therapy.
Will it force me to do scary things?
No. Challenges scale with your consistency. You start gentle. You can skip any challenge that doesn't fit. Pro users get unlimited skips and can browse the full library to pick what fits today.
What if I miss a day?
Your streak holds if you show up the next day. If you break a streak past 3 days, the app offers a $0.99 streak rescue so you don't lose weeks of momentum over a single bad week.
Is it free?
Yes. The daily challenge, streaks, the widget, and one notification a day are free forever. Pro is $4.99/month or $29.99/year and unlocks browsing the full 580+ library, unlimited skips, and removes ads.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your streak, history, and preferences stay on your device. No account, no cloud, no social feed. The app talks to AdMob and AppsFlyer only if you grant App Tracking Transparency consent — otherwise nothing leaves the phone.
Is there a home screen widget?
Yes. Small or medium tile showing today's challenge and your streak. Tap to mark complete without opening the app.
Does it work for shy kids or teens?
The app is rated 12+. Some challenges reference real adult social situations (work meetings, dating-adjacent situations) so it's not built for younger kids. Teens and adults both use it.
