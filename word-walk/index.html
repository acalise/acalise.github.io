What Is It

A 140-year-old puzzle, reborn for iPhone

Word ladders were invented by Lewis Carroll in 1877. You start with one word, and you walk it to another by changing a single letter at each step. Every rung has to be a valid English word. It's simple, addictive, and brutally good for vocabulary.

How To Play

Word ladders in 3 steps

If you can change one letter at a time, you already know how to play. Word Walk takes about 10 seconds to learn and months to master.

1

Start with the given word

Every puzzle hands you a start word and a target. Example: CAT at the top, DOG at the bottom.

2

Change one letter at a time

Swap a single letter to form a new valid word. CAT → COT. Then COT → COG. Every rung has to be a real English word.

3

Reach the target in as few steps as possible

Finish in the minimum number of moves to earn three stars. Share your score and see how friends did.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know about word ladders and Word Walk.

What is Word Walk?
Word Walk is a daily word ladder puzzle game for iPhone. You start with one word and transform it into a target word by changing a single letter at a time, with every step forming a valid English word.
What is a word ladder?
A word ladder is a classic word puzzle invented by Lewis Carroll in 1877. You transform one word into another by changing one letter at each step, and every intermediate word must be a real word. Example: CAT → COT → COG → DOG.
Is Word Walk like Wordle?
Word Walk and Wordle are both daily word games, but they're different puzzles. Wordle is a single-word guessing game. Word Walk is a transformation puzzle where you build a chain of valid words from a start to a target. Fans of Wordle usually love Word Walk because it's a deeper, more strategic daily workout.
Does Word Walk work offline?
Yes. All puzzle gameplay works fully offline. You only need an internet connection to share your score or compare with friends.
Is Word Walk free?
Yes, Word Walk is free to play. You can optionally remove ads with a one-time $1.99 in-app purchase. No subscription.
How do I get better at word ladders?
Work backward from the target. Focus on moveable consonants. Learn common vowel swaps (A↔O, E↔I). Our strategy guide covers it in depth.
Is it good for kids?
Yes. Word Walk is rated 4+. It's a fantastic vocabulary builder for kids learning to spell, and a sharp warmup for adults. No violence, no chat, no social features kids shouldn't see.
