FiberUp Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

FiberUp ("we", "the app") is a fiber tracking app for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Storage

All of your fiber tracking data — including daily logs, food entries, goals, and preferences — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your data never leaves your device except as described below.

No Account Required

FiberUp does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Advertising

FiberUp uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking). For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

FiberUp offers an optional "Remove Ads" in-app purchase processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Camera

FiberUp requests camera access solely to scan barcodes on packaged food products. Camera data is processed on-device in real time and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Notifications

FiberUp may request permission to send local notifications as daily reminders. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Third-Party Services

FiberUp uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements
  • Open Food Facts — for looking up nutritional data from scanned barcodes (barcode numbers are sent to their public API)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties, except as described in the Advertising and Third-Party Services sections above.

Children's Privacy

FiberUp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all FiberUp data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at acalisedev@gmail.com.
