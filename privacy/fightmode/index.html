FightMode Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Overview

FightMode ("we", "the app") is an iOS app that blocks distracting apps and lets you earn screen time by fighting boxing or kickboxing rounds. It optionally records your rounds for AI coaching analysis. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Storage

Your data — earned minutes, streaks, round history, settings, and blocked-app selections — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a database of your activity. Your data never leaves your device except as described below (AI round analysis, purchase verification, anonymous analytics, and advertising attribution).

No Account Required

FightMode does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The name you optionally enter during setup stays on your device and is only used to personalize on-screen text.

Screen Time and App Blocking

FightMode uses Apple's Screen Time frameworks (Family Controls, Managed Settings, and Device Activity) to block the apps you choose. Apple provides your app selections to FightMode as opaque tokens — we cannot see which apps you selected, and neither can anyone else. These tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted anywhere. Screen Time authorization is handled entirely by Apple.

Camera, Microphone, and Round Recording

FightMode requests camera and microphone access only if you turn on recording for a round. Recording is optional — you can fight every round without it. Recorded videos are saved locally on your device. You can also choose to save a round video to your Photos library, which uses add-only access (the app cannot read your existing photos).

AI Round Analysis

If you request an AI scorecard for a recorded round, the video — along with the round's style, target, and the callouts your coach gave — is sent to our backend service for analysis. The backend uses Google Gemini to evaluate your technique and generate the scorecard. After processing:

  • The video is not stored on our servers beyond the duration of the request.
  • Temporary files created during AI processing are deleted automatically within 48 hours.
  • The AI provider does not retain your video for training purposes (per our service agreements).
  • The scorecard is returned to your device and stored locally with the round.
  • We do not associate the video with directly identifying information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Coach Voices

Coach callouts during rounds are played from audio files bundled inside the app. No audio is generated, streamed, or transmitted during a round.

Subscriptions and Purchases

FightMode offers an optional auto-renewable subscription. All purchases and billing are handled entirely through Apple's App Store; we do not collect or store any payment information. Subscription status is verified through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and your entitlement status; no name, email address, phone number, or payment details are shared by FightMode. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles purchases.

Notifications

FightMode may request permission to send local notifications, such as reminders to fight. Notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

No Ads Inside the App

FightMode does not display any ads and does not show you third-party advertising while you use it. We do, however, run ads for FightMode on other platforms to reach new users, and we measure how those ads perform as described in the two sections below.

Analytics

FightMode uses PostHog, a third-party analytics service, to understand how the app is used and to improve it. We record anonymous events such as which screens you open, when a round starts and finishes, and when the paywall is shown. PostHog also captures anonymized session replays of on-screen activity to help us find and fix usability problems. Text you type is masked and is never recorded. This data is tied to a random, anonymous identifier, not to your name. Analytics data is processed on PostHog servers in the United States.

Attribution and Advertising

We run ads for FightMode on other platforms, including Apple Search Ads, Meta, and TikTok. To understand which ads bring new users to the app, we use AppsFlyer, a third-party attribution service, together with your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). When you first open the app we ask permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, FightMode works exactly the same and installs are measured using Apple's privacy-preserving SKAdNetwork instead. For our Apple Search Ads campaigns we use Apple's AdServices attribution, which does not identify you across other companies' apps or websites. Device identifiers and install data may be shared with these advertising and attribution partners solely to measure the performance of our own ads. We do not sell your data.

You can turn off tracking at any time in iOS Settings, under Privacy and Security, then Tracking.

Third-Party Services

FightMode uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini — for analyzing round videos you submit for an AI scorecard (videos are sent for analysis and not stored)
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management (an anonymous app user ID and entitlement status are shared)
  • PostHog — for anonymous product analytics and session replays used to improve the app
  • AppsFlyer — for measuring which ad campaigns bring new users to the app
  • Apple (Search Ads and SKAdNetwork) — for privacy-preserving measurement of our own advertising

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with third parties only as described above: with Google Gemini for AI round analysis, with RevenueCat for subscription management, with PostHog for anonymous analytics, and with AppsFlyer and our advertising platforms (Apple Search Ads, Meta, and TikTok) solely to measure the performance of our own ads.

Children's Privacy

FightMode is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the app. Round videos you saved to your Photos library remain in Photos until you delete them there. Anonymous records held by our service providers (RevenueCat, PostHog, and AppsFlyer) are retained in accordance with each provider's data retention policy. You can stop advertising attribution at any time by declining tracking in iOS Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
