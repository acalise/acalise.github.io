Somleng Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Overview

Somleng ("we", "the app") is an automation tool operated by Anthony Calise that publishes AI-generated Khmer music videos to the Somleng Facebook Page and YouTube channel owned by the same operator. This policy explains what data the app handles.

No Data Collected from Viewers

Somleng does not collect, store, or share any personal data from people who watch, like, comment on, or otherwise interact with the content it publishes. Viewers interact with Facebook and YouTube directly; Facebook's and YouTube's own privacy policies apply to any data those platforms collect.

Data the App Uses

To publish content, the app uses access tokens belonging to the operator's own Facebook Page and YouTube channel. These tokens are stored locally on the operator's computer and are never shared with third parties. The app does not read viewer comments, messages, follower lists, or any other audience data.

No Account Required

Somleng does not require viewers to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

No Analytics or Tracking

The app does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It does not run in users' browsers or devices.

AI-Generated Content

Music, lyrics, vocals, and cover art published through Somleng are generated by AI models. No real person's voice, likeness, or personal data is used to generate the content.

Data Retention and Deletion

The app retains no viewer data, so there is nothing for viewers to request deletion of. For questions about published videos, please use the contact information below.

Children's Privacy

Somleng publishes general-audience music content and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact Anthony Calise at anthonydcalise@gmail.com.
