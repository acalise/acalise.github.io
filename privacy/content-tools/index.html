Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy applies to internal content management software ("the software") used by its operator to publish content to social media accounts owned by the operator. The software is not offered to the public and does not collect data from end users.

Data Collection

The software does not collect, store, or transmit personal information from any third party. It operates exclusively on content created and owned by the operator and publishes that content to platforms where the operator holds an account.

Data Usage

Authentication tokens used to access social media platform APIs are stored locally and used solely to publish content authorized by the operator. No user-facing accounts, sign-ins, or data uploads are involved.

Third-Party Services

The software interacts with public APIs provided by social media platforms (such as Meta's Graph API). Use of those APIs is governed by their respective platform terms and privacy policies.

Data Retention

No third-party data is retained. Content published by the software is governed by the policies of the platform on which it is published.

Children's Privacy

The software is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

For questions regarding this policy, contact the operator at the email associated with the platform account.
