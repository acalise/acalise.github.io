T-Score Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

Overview

T-Score ("we", "the app") is a testosterone habit tracking app for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Storage

All of your tracking data — including daily check-ins, scores, streaks, bloodwork entries, supplement logs, and preferences — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your data never leaves your device except as described below.

No Account Required

T-Score does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Apple HealthKit

T-Score can optionally read sleep and workout data from Apple Health to auto-fill your daily check-in. This feature requires your explicit permission and is only available to Pro subscribers.

Your HealthKit data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never sent to any server or third party
  • Never used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Never sold or shared

You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > T-Score.

Subscriptions

T-Score offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions managed through Apple's App Store. Purchase and billing information is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details. Subscription status is verified through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and subscription status — no personal information is shared.

Advertising

T-Score uses Google AdMob to display ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking). Pro subscribers do not see ads.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Push Notifications

T-Score may send optional local push notifications for daily check-in reminders and weekly summaries. These are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or in Settings > Notifications > T-Score.

Analytics

T-Score does not use any analytics or crash reporting services. We do not collect usage data, crash logs, or behavioral analytics.

Children's Privacy

T-Score is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
