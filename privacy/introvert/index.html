Introvert Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Overview

Introvert ("we", "the app") is a daily confidence-building challenge app for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Storage

All of your data — including completed challenges, streaks, category preferences, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your data never leaves your device except as described below.

No Account Required

Introvert does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Subscriptions

Introvert offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions and one-time purchases managed through Apple's App Store. Purchase and billing information is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details. Subscription status is verified through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management service. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID and subscription status — no personal information is shared.

Advertising

Introvert uses Google AdMob to display ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data related to ad interactions

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking). Pro subscribers do not see ads.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Attribution & Analytics

Introvert uses AppsFlyer to measure the performance of our advertising campaigns on platforms such as Meta and TikTok. AppsFlyer may process:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
  • IP address
  • Install and in-app event timestamps
  • Subscription purchase events (via App Store)

This data is used solely to attribute app installs to the ad campaign that drove them and to measure conversion. No personally identifiable information is transmitted. You can opt out of tracking through your device's privacy settings.

For more information, see AppsFlyer's Privacy Policy.

Push Notifications

Introvert may send optional local push notifications as daily challenge reminders. These are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or in Settings > Notifications > Introvert.

Widget

Introvert provides an optional home-screen widget that displays your daily challenge and streak. Widget data is shared between the app and the widget using Apple's App Groups — it remains entirely on your device.

Crash Reporting

Introvert does not use any third-party crash reporting services. Apple may collect anonymized crash reports through its standard iOS diagnostics if you have opted in at the system level.

Children's Privacy

Introvert is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
