FAQ

Questions, answered

What is FightMode?

FightMode is an app blocker for iPhone that makes you earn your screen time. You choose which apps to block, and to open them you win a 60-second boxing or kickboxing round. A coach calls out real combos, you throw them, and winning banks minutes of screen time. It's the first app blocker built specifically for fighters.

How does it block apps?

FightMode uses Apple's Screen Time (Family Controls) framework to shield the apps you pick. Tap a blocked app and you see a lock screen instead. Spend banked minutes to unlock it - and earn those minutes by winning rounds.

Do I need boxing experience?

No. Three difficulty levels start at a first-timer pace, and your coach calls every combo out loud so you always know what to throw. Shadowbox with no equipment or hit a bag - your call.

How is it different from Pushscroll?

Pushscroll and Repscroll make you do pushups or squats. FightMode uses boxing and kickboxing rounds, adds live coach callouts, and gives you an AI scorecard on your technique. Same Screen Time blocking engine, a far better unlock task.

What's the AI scorecard?

Record a round on your front camera and your corner reviews the tape - a score, a correction and a drill for each technique your coach actually called. The first analysis is free.
