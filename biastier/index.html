FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything to know before you build your first list.

What is K-Pop Bias Battle?
An iPhone app that ranks K-pop idols through head-to-head matchups. You pick between two idols at a time, and a smart ELO algorithm uses your picks to build a tier list. Faster and more accurate than dragging idols around manually.
Which groups are included?
99 idols across 15 of the biggest K-pop groups: BLACKPINK, BTS, NewJeans, TWICE, Stray Kids, ENHYPEN, IVE, aespa, LE SSERAFIM, SEVENTEEN, ATEEZ, RIIZE, TXT, ILLIT, and TWS.
How is this different from a web sorter?
Web sorters work but they're awkward on phone, lose your progress, and have no way to share a clean image. K-Pop Bias Battle is a native iPhone app — fast, persistent, and built around shareable tier-list output for TikTok and IG.
Is it free?
Yes. You can rank any single group for free. The Pro upgrade is a one-time $4.99 purchase that unlocks all-time, generation, and gender scopes, every theme pack, and removes ads. No subscription.
Do I need to create an account?
No. There's no signup, no login, no email. Your tier lists live entirely on your device.
Where do the idol photos come from?
All photos are sourced from Wikimedia Commons, which hosts freely licensed images of public figures. We never store, host, or modify the original photos.
Can I edit my tier list after the algorithm builds it?
Yes. Tap any idol to move them to a different tier, or long-press to drag and drop. Your manual moves are saved with the rest of the list.
Why are there ads?
Free users see a small banner at the top of each screen and a full-screen ad after each completed ranking session. Pro removes both. The ads keep the app free for casual fans while still letting power users buy out the friction for $4.99 once.
